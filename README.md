# 🌍 EcoLens – AI-Powered Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform

EcoLens is an intelligent sustainability platform designed to help individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights, AI-powered recommendations, eco-friendly route planning, and behavioral sustainability analytics.

Unlike traditional carbon calculators that provide static estimates, EcoLens combines Artificial Intelligence, sustainability tracking, environmental analytics, and gamification to create a complete Sustainability Decision Support System that encourages long-term positive environmental habits.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Most individuals are unaware of how their daily activities contribute to carbon emissions.

Existing sustainability tools often suffer from:

- Static carbon calculations
- Generic environmental advice
- Lack of personalization
- Poor user engagement
- Minimal habit-building mechanisms
- No intelligent sustainability guidance

EcoLens addresses these challenges by transforming environmental awareness into measurable and actionable improvements through AI.

## 🔄 Application Workflow

1. Users log sustainability activities.
2. Backend validates and processes activity data.
3. Carbon footprint calculations are generated.
4. Data is securely stored in Firestore.
5. AI analyzes sustainability patterns.
6. Gemini generates personalized recommendations.
7. Google Maps provides route intelligence.
8. Dashboard visualizes environmental impact and progress.
